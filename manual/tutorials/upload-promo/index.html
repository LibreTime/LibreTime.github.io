How to Upload a Promo

  • YouTube video

Step-by-step Text Based

Audience: DJs

  1. Record your promo and export it as a mp3. Note: if you can set the genre metadata to promo you can skip the final step below
  2. Login to LibreTime with your DJ username
  3. Click Upload and drag or click and browse to upload the track.
  4. Let the track upload. Once it is done and is shown as imported on the right, click Tracks
  5. Find the track in the list
  6. If it is not at the top click the Uploaded column until it sorts by the newest tracks first.
  7. If Uploaded doesn’t show up click Columns to the top right scroll down and click the box next to Uploaded
  8. Now click Uploaded twice and it should display your track at the top.
  9. Click the box next your track and click the Edit button above the track listing.
  10. A box saying Editing “track name” should show up on the right.
  11. Find promo type in Genre (or whatever metadata classification your station uses)
  12. Scroll down and click Save
  13. If you match the metadata requirements for the station smart block then your track will be included in the track list it pulls from.
